According to Jiménez-Sandoval, “the secret is in the sauce.” The thick, golden apple pie sauce, the product of boiled down unfiltered apple juice, serves as a secret ingredient in the delectable finished product: a golden, delicious apple pie fit for the warmest of Thanksgiving tables. It took over 12 years for Jiménez-Sandoval to perfect his recipe, which he is now sharing with the community.
